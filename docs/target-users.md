# Target Users

## Primary Audience

MicroCRM is built for small Instagram-based businesses, specifically:

- **Small Instagram shops** — any niche product sold via DMs and Stories
- **Handmade stores** — creators who take custom orders and need to track each client carefully
- **Beauty stores** — nail salons, lash artists, makeup sellers who book appointments via Instagram

## Team Size

- Teams of **1 to 5 workers**
- Solo sellers managing everything themselves
- Small teams where one person handles chats, another handles orders

## Key Characteristics

These users typically:
- Receive 10–100+ DMs per day
- Have no dedicated CRM or support tool
- Manage orders via screenshots or memory
- Lose clients due to delayed or missed replies
