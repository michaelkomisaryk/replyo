# Problem and Solution

## Problem

Small Instagram shops face real daily challenges:

- **Lost messages** — DMs get buried under new ones; clients are left waiting or forgotten entirely
- **Missed clients** — no system to track who needs a reply, who placed an order, and who was never answered
- **Hard communication** — switching between Instagram, notes apps, and spreadsheets creates confusion and lost revenue

## Solution

MicroCRM solves these problems with:

- **Chat management** — all Instagram DMs in one place, organized by status and priority
- **Message organization** — clear labels and filters so nothing gets lost
- **Smart priorities** — automatic sorting of chats based on urgency, order status, and client activity
