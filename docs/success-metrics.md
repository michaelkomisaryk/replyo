# Success Metrics

How we measure whether MicroCRM is actually working:

## Faster Replies
- Average first response time drops below 30 minutes
- "Waiting for Reply" chats older than 2 hours decrease week-over-week

## Fewer Lost Clients
- Zero unanswered chats older than 24 hours
- Reduction in clients who stop responding after not getting a reply

## Daily Usage
- Team logs in and uses the dashboard every working day
- No one falls back to managing DMs directly in Instagram

## Better Sales Process
- More orders tracked from first message to delivery
- Managers can see full client history without asking teammates
- Order completion rate improves over first 30 days of use
